About Me

Hello, my name is Joe Barbieri. First off, thank-you for taking the time to check out my digital portfolio. Most of the work here was complied throughout my undergraduate years at UC Denver where I graduated with a degree in writing.

In my studies, I covered a wide array of writing styles and formats. Though I am most practiced in journalistic writing and creative writing, I was also taught basic skills in both technical writing and scientific writing. I am familiar in writing APA, MLA, and Chicago style papers. I am confident in my flexibility and adaptability across any form of writing and I am always seeking a position where I can apply my skills effectively.
